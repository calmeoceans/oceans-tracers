Threats to Marine Ecosystems

Overfishing

Unsustainable fishing practices are depleting fish populations faster than they can reproduce, disrupting marine food webs and ecosystems.

Climate Change

Rising ocean temperatures and acidification are causing coral bleaching, shifting species distributions, and altering marine habitats.

Pollution

Plastic waste, agricultural runoff, and industrial chemicals are contaminating marine environments, harming wildlife and ecosystems.
